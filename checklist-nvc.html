NVC Document Checklist

CEAC Submission — before you can be Documentarily Qualified (DQ)
londonvisatracker.github.io
v1.4 · Apr 2026
Before you start
  • Case created and NVC Welcome Letter received Contains your NVC Case Number and Invoice ID — save both
  • CEAC access confirmed at ceac.state.gov/IV/Login.aspx
  • IV fee ($325) paid and showing PAID
  • Affidavit of Support fee ($120) paid and showing PAID
Rule: You cannot upload documents until both fees are paid and cleared.
Financial documents (petitioner)
  • Form I-864 — Affidavit of Support Wet signed — NO digital signatures
  • IRS Tax Transcript Most recent year — from IRS website, not a copy of your return
  • W-2s / 1099s
  • Proof of current employment Employment letter or contract
  • Recent pay stubs
Petitioner must meet 125% of US Federal Poverty Guidelines based on household size. If income is insufficient, irregular, asset-heavy, or earned abroad — a joint sponsor is required.
Financial documents (joint sponsor)

Complete only if a joint sponsor is required

  • Separate Form I-864 (signed)
  • IRS Tax Transcript
  • W-2s / 1099s
  • Proof of US status US passport or green card
  • Proof of US domicile
  • Proof of income: W2/1099 employees: employment letter + pay stubs Self-employed: 12 months business bank statements + evidence of business Retired: evidence of retirement income source
London-specific risk: Asset-only or borderline income cases frequently result in 221(g) delays. If there is any doubt, secure a joint sponsor early.
Civil documents (beneficiary)
Required documents vary by visa category (spouse, child, parent)
  • Birth Certificate Long form with parent names
  • Marriage Certificate If applicable
  • UK ACRO Police Certificate Required for UK residence
  • Police Certificates from all other qualifying countries Check reciprocity schedule for rules
  • Passport biographic page Must be valid — preferably until naturalization
If using a PCC placeholder — you MUST upload the actual certificate by interview day or risk 221(g).
Conditional / case-specific

Include only if applicable

  • Divorce Decrees All prior marriages
  • Death Certificates Former spouses
  • Court / criminal records
  • Prison records
  • Military records
Upload and submit
  • Each document placed in the correct CEAC category
  • Clear, legible scans — all pages included
  • Max file size 4MB per upload
  • No duplicates or missing pages
  • Do NOT mail originals to NVC — bring them to interview
  • Click Submit Documents — uploading alone does not enter you into the review queue
Recommended naming convention:
[CASE NUMBER]_[DOCUMENT TYPE]_[NAME]
Example: LNDXXXX_BirthCert_Beneficiary

Top failure points from real cases

  • Uploading documents to the wrong CEAC category
  • Missing police certificates for countries lived in
  • Incomplete financial evidence — assuming assets are sufficient without a sponsor
  • Submitting before fully ready — every RFE adds ~2 weeks
  • I-864 digitally signed instead of wet signed
  • PCC placeholder not replaced before interview
