Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy explains how americanvisaguide.com collects, uses, and stores data. This site is a community resource for US family immigrant visa applicants and is not affiliated with the US government or any embassy.

Who we are

americanvisaguide.com is operated by Erin Dyer Saxon. You can contact us at AmericanVisaGuideInfo@gmail.com.

What data we collect

Timeline form submissions

If you submit your visa timeline via the FillOut form, we collect: your Discord handle, email address (optional), visa category, embassy, and immigration process dates (I-130 approval, NVC stages, interview date, etc.). This data is stored in a Supabase database and used to calculate the community statistics displayed on this site. Your Discord handle may be displayed publicly in the "This week" section of the data page.

Analytics — Microsoft Clarity

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use the site. Clarity records anonymised session data including mouse movements, clicks, and scrolling behaviour. Clarity may use cookies. No personally identifiable information is captured. See Microsoft's privacy statement for details.

No other tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, advertising cookies, or any other third-party tracking beyond Microsoft Clarity.

How we use your data

  • Timeline data is used only to calculate aggregate statistics displayed on this site
  • We do not sell, share, or use data for advertising
  • Your Discord handle may appear in the weekly activity section of the data page
  • Email addresses submitted via the form are not displayed publicly

Data retention

Timeline data is retained indefinitely to maintain historical accuracy of the community statistics. If you would like your data removed, email AmericanVisaGuideInfo@gmail.com with your Discord handle and we will delete your record.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact AmericanVisaGuideInfo@gmail.com.

Cookies

This site may set cookies via Microsoft Clarity for session recording purposes. No essential cookies are used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third-party services

  • FillOut — form submissions. See FillOut's privacy policy.
  • Supabase — database hosting. See Supabase's privacy policy.
  • Vercel — site hosting. See Vercel's privacy policy.
  • Microsoft Clarity — session analytics. See Microsoft's privacy statement.

Contact
