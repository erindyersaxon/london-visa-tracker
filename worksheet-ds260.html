DS-260 Preparation Worksheet

Immigrant Visa Application — Complete before logging into CEAC
londonvisatracker.github.io
v1.4 · Apr 2026
⚠️ CEAC times out after ~10 minutes of inactivity and is very unreliable. Complete this worksheet in full before logging in. Once you submit the DS-260 you cannot edit it — double-check every answer. The DS-260 confirmation page must be printed and brought to your medical and interview.
1. Personal information (beneficiary)
2. Address and contact information
Platform and username for each. Requested but optional.
3. Passport / Travel document
4. Family information — parents
5. Family information — spouse (petitioner)
6. Family information — children
If a child is a US citizen (e.g. via CRBA), answer "No" to "Is the child immigrating with you?" — they are returning as a citizen, not immigrating as a visa applicant.
7. Previous US travel
8. US contact information
9. Work / Education / Training
10. Security and background

Answer Yes/No to each category. Explain any "Yes" answers fully — be truthful and consistent with all previous records.

11. Vaccinations
If you plan to get missing vaccines at the medical exam, select "Other" on the vaccination question and explain in the dialogue box that you will receive all required vaccines at the medical examination.
12. Social Security Number
When you submit the DS-260, print the confirmation page immediately. You will need it at both your medical appointment and your visa interview.
